Education Startups List

Recently, I search so many times about education startups to learn from them. This post list all startups that I am interested. I categories them by an answer on quora.

New Institutions:

online high schools, colleges, certification programs, enterprise training programs, and test preparation programs, experimental online programs, innovative offline programs. An example of a new institution for experimental online programs for universities: 2tor

Learning Management companies: web-based tools, analytics and reporting systems, learning management systems, corporate learning solutions.

Online Content: online video in forms of TED.com, from Khan Academy. free content from top universities, open-licensed content, wikis, content libraries, digital textbooks,

Networks and Marketplaces: tutor and training listings, paid/free online video and live training, college choice companies, online resume programs.

Live Training and Tutoring: streaming lectures and conferences, specialized teaching tools like Supercoolschool, tutor-student networks. Learner Tools: online backchannels, memorization help, mobile learning apps such as StudyBlue and test prep programs. Collaborative Learning: online study groups, modern question and answering platforms like Quora.com, language and teacher communities. Funding and Payments: A problem that does not have too many solutions is college funding but Enzi.org and GradeFund are making efforts to improve financial questions surrounding education. Hardware for Education: One Laptop Per Child and tablet computers.
